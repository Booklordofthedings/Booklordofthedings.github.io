Me about my level of written english

My main knowledge of the english language comes from the internet and not from school.
This is probably a fairly common for people that have english as their secondary language nowadays, however for me there is the additional problem of actually learning almost nothing during english class.
My teacher was horrible at teaching and basically the entire class agreed to it, so my writing suffered.

After basically being adicted to watching yt and at some point switching to watching it mainly in english, my level of english started to improve, with a big focus there being spoken english.

I am fairly good at speaking fluently and understandable, but if you where to analyze my writing you would likely find that I actually tend to write the way that I speak, wich causes alot of wordrepeats and way too simple or colloquial sentences.
I know that this is a problem and I am slowly trying to improve that, and a partiall way of trying to do so will be by writing more blogposts on here, as this allows me to practice by doing something that I actually like instead of the usual writing I have to do in my current englishclass.

So my goal is to post more on here and improving my written english

lets see if I ever end up doing it.
