Why the name ?

A short history of my online username

Booklordofthedings

A unique name isnt it ? But how did it come that way ?

Well the word Book was in there since the beginning. My first online representation was made by me together with my parents and I didnt really just wanted to use my full name, but rather I wanted to use something interesting. And as my main hobby during that time was reading and I wanted something that was cool (english words where cool at the time) so I used Book and my Firstname.

However during the time after I was really into Minecraft and loved watching it/wanted to make my own channel. That was the reason I renamed most of my stuff to Bookcrafter_LP. I know how cringy that was but it was mainly a phase. At some point while watching a stream a streamer called my name out, and since I agreed to not liking it at the time I ended up making a new online persona. Also I had just watched one of the lotr movies.

Book (Reference to my hobby and old persona) + lordofthe (lotr reference) + dings (german word for “thing” and also very similar to rings)

So I had my unique and non cringe name, but how did I come up with the Lybry part that I often use ?

This was due to a limitation with the name system on switch. “Booklordofthedings” was way too long and Book wasnt interesting enough for me, so I used lbry.Book (similar to pkmn on pokemon cards, but with library). But since I was playing brawlhalla at the time I often ended up changing my name and because of a spelling error on my side I ended up with lybry.book wich I liked enough to just keep it. Now alot of things that have to do with me use lybry as a name.
